DANGERDice: A Fair Game, Sometimes

A rogue-like game inspired by TinyDiceDungeons. Game was written in Python (pygame), graphics were made in Photoshop, and music was made with FL Studio. Only Windows is supported currently.

Play as an unassuming square, defeating people that get in your way with sheer luck. Level up, make money, and collect dice to turn the odds in your favor on this strange journey!

r/curious

A simple web application written with vanilla CSS and Javascript with the goal of learning more about using APIs.

The app tells you what percentage of posts on a certain subreddit mention some topic or keyword. It also serves as a bare bones Reddit viewer.
